More About Charlie Farquharson's Histry Of Canada

What can one say about good ole Don. He brought to Canadian history what was so typically Canadian - humour. With tongue firmly in cheek, he quickly goes through the short history of Canada. The way he writes makes one want to read aloud and hopefully with an audience. I was still in school when I got this book and it's companion (Jogfree of Canda). I enjoyed reading them then and still enjoy going back and re-reading them now. Charlie Farquharson was a well known name in our house and I hope he lives on for many many more years to come.
