The password is a flicker of an eyelash. Adrienne Rich, Selected Poems //

This is a wonderful parallel translation into English of the selected poems of Pacheco up to the mid-1980s. I recommend it highly for those who read Spanish well enough to get the flavour of the original but want an inspiring English translation La traductora resta mucha fuerza plstica a los poemas. Algunos son demasiado explcitos, narrativos y poco sugerentes, pero otros logran que los elementos tengan funcin fetiche, simblica, universalizante o arquetpica.
